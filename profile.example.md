## Opener style

- First 20 characters should connect to the JD action, not introduce yourself.
- Use one most relevant fact, not a full resume.
- 45-120 Chinese characters is usually enough.
- Do not ask salary or benefits in the opener.
- Do not claim you can do something unless it is in the facts above.
